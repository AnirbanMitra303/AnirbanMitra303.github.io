Hi there, I'm Anirban Mitra

I currently work as a Senior Statistician in Johnson & Johnson Pvt. Ltd. I obtained a Ph.D. in Statistics (Spring, 2023) from the University of Pittsburgh under supervision of Professor Joshua Cape.
My primary research area is Networks, mostly in application to Neuroscience. Although my research has been largely focused on classical Network theory, currently I have been learning Graph Neural Networks, specially Graph Convolutions. I am also interested in Multivariate Analysis in general. Previously, for various projects, I had dipped my hands into Experimental Design, Additive models, Time Series analysis, Signal Processing, Non-parametric theory and Graphical models.
